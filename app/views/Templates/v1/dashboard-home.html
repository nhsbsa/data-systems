Data Services - Help with Health Costs

The NHS Business Services Authority (NHSBSA) administers a range of services to help people with their health costs, including help with:

  • NHS prescription costs
  • NHS dental costs
  • sight tests, glasses and contact lenses
  • travel to receive NHS treatment
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports

Full detauks of these schemes are available on the NHS Help with Health Costs webpage.

These statistics provide data related to these certificate schemes. Data covers the financial years 2018/19 to 2023/24, where the financial year runs from April 1 to March 31.

Users and uses

This publication can have a wide range of users, including:

  • informing government or local NHS policy
  • allowing public scrutiny of national and regional prescribing habits
  • responding to queries made under the Freedom of Information (FOI) act
  • supporting resolution of ad-hoc enquiries

The NHSBSA administer services providing help with health costs. The data that forms the basis of these statistics is collected as a by-product of this process.
