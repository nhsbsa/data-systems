eDEN Prototype

eDEN, an online application, offers several functionalities to streamline dental data management. Users can swiftly access dental data, benefiting from faster retrieval times. The platform also provides user-friendly report management, enabling efficient handling and interpretation of data. Additionally, eDEN allows users to refresh and export both dashboards and flat data tables, facilitating easy extraction and analysis. Its interface includes visualizations for a clear and comprehensive view of the data.
