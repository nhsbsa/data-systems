Welcome

Dear Colleague,

We hope you find accessing information via the eDEN system will enable faster and easier access to the data you need.

The eDEN system contains a number of dashboards, presenting information in a variety of ways, combining the best elements of the Year End and Vital Signs reports. To access the dashboard, please click the links opposite this news story.

The system is designed to evolve over time with feedback from its users. Please do make use of the link at the right-hand side of this page to leave us your feedback on how the system is performing, any technical or suspected data issues or any extra data you would like to see in the future.

For more information on eDEN including training materials, please see our website here.
