ePACT2 Prototype

ePACT2 provides authorized users with access to prescription data, enabling them to explore interactive reports and dashboards conveniently. Users can delve into high-level data summaries and examine individual prescription items and patient-level details. Additionally, the platform allows users to create custom analyses and easily export data from reports and dashboards.
