Data services - ePACT2

Us this service to access prescription data

The ePACT2 service will allow you to access interactive and easy to use reports and dashboards, view high level data summaries down to individual prescriotion items and patient level detail, build bespoke analysis and exporting data.

Understanding ePACT2 in data service

Data is available 6 weeks after the dispensing month.

ePACT2 contains data down to single prescriotion level. If you have access to this level of data, remember that you cannot share confidential data outside of your organisation. For this reason, you should have read and agreed to the ePACT2 User Agreement as part of the registration process.
