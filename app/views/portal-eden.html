Data services - eDEN

Use this service to access dental data.

eDEN is an online product designed to streamline your dental data experience. With eDEN, you can access dental information, enabling faster retrieval of essential data. The platform offers user-friendly reports for efficient management, empowering users to navigate and interpret information effortlessly.

Additionally, eDEN provides the functionality to refresh and export dashboards and flat data tables, ensuring dynamic and up-to-date insights. Engage with data visualizations seamlessly, enhancing your overall experience in managing dental information through this comprehensive online application.
