Data services - ePACT2

Use this service to access prescription data.

The ePACT2 service will allow you to access interactive and easy to use reports and dashboards, view high level data summaries down to individual prescription items and patient level detail, build bespoke analysis and export data.

Understanding ePACT2 in data services

Data is available 6 weeks after the dispensing month

ePACT2 contains data down to single prescription level. If you have access to this level of data, remember that you cannot share confidential data outside of your organisation. For this reason, you should have read and agreed to the ePACT2 User Agreement as part of the registration process.
